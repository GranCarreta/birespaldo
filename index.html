Batch Image Resizing Made Easy

BIRME is a flexible and easy to use batch image resizer. It can resize your images to any specific dimensions and crop your images proportionately if necessary. It's an online tool and that means you don't need to download or install anything on your computer. BIRME is absolutely free to use.
